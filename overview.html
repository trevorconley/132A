Why use a wireframe tool?

Plan Ahead

Are you a frustrated Web Developer that spends an obscene amount of time designing several different drafts before finally coming to a design you sort of like?

Then a wireframe tool is for you! Cut your time dramatically by creating a mockup design of your website. Once you have the design, then you can move forward to implementing the design how you wish.

Cost

Design the layout before investing resources.

Time

Save time by having a plan before implementation.

Simple

Focus solely on the structural design before adding color and content.
