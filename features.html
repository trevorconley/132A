Group challenges:

The first challenge as a group was to figure out how to meet up to complete the first assignment. We matched schedules and we were still unable to find reasonable times to collaborate. As engineers, we came together and tackled the problem with technology. We created a google group, communicated through email, coded on a repository, and chatted through skype. In result, this convenient solution allowed us to overcome the challenges we faced; that is, we were all able to work from home with our own schedules.
